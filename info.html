Due to the COVID-19 outbreak, the final few months of the Spring 2020 semester had to be canceled for in-school learning. Sadly, the Hamilton Finance Club had to go down with it, and our meeting in early March ended up being our final time seeing one another for the rest of the year. For the 2020-2021 school year, the Hamilton Finance Club is returning in style. Meeting dates and other important events are listed on the calendar, and connecting to a Zoom call is as simple as clicking on this link. We hope to see you all soon!
