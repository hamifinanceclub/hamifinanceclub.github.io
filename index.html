Money is everywhere.

We're here to help you learn how to manage and invest it

Our goal is to spread basic financial literacy to Hamilton High School students.

At first, the Hamilton Finance Club was formed as a group of students eager to practice investing through a Stock Market Challenge. While planning the first competition, we couldn't help but realize the opportunity to educate our peers on an essential life skill. As students begin to work, take out loans, and use credit cards, having a fundamental understanding of money and its seemingly endless functions becomes a necessity. Together, we've built a club where any Hamilton student can expand their knowledge and contribute to the conversation, and we hope this website serves as a continuation of that philosophy.
